How to Play:

  • Guess the hidden word by entering one letter at a time
  • You have a limited number of incorrect guesses
  • Each correct guess reveals the letter in the word
  • Each incorrect guess reduces your remaining attempts
  • Complete the word before running out of attempts to win!
